About Our Company

We are a leading music company committed to nurturing talents and delivering unforgettable musical experiences. Since our founding in 2020, we have grown from a small independent label to a trusted name in the music industry.

Our team consists of passionate musicians, producers, and creative professionals who work together to create world-class music productions, events, and digital content for audiences everywhere.

With headquarters in Tangerang, Pati, Salatiga, and more, we collaborate with artists across Indonesia and beyond. Our dedication to creativity, quality, and innovation has made us a go-to partner for aspiring musicians and brands seeking impactful music solutions.
